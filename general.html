General Education Resources

Whether you are still a student or an adult, learning is something that transcends the ages. While everyone is at home, here are some amazing resources to make the most out of your summer!

Skillshare

Skillshare is a large marketplace focused on creative education. Topics include anything from graphic design and music production to fine art and cooking.
"Click here to go to Skillshare!"

LinkedIn Learning

With over 1,000 business courses on topics like project management, web development, and digital marketing, LinkedIn Learning is an excellent platform for working professionals looking to keep their skills up-to-date.
"Click here to go to LinkedIn Learning!"

Udemy

Udemy is an online course platform marketplace with over 24 million students, 35,000 instructors, and 80,000+ courses.
"Click here to go to Udemy!"

General Assembly

Master new skills in design, marketing, technology, and data — online.
Click here to go to General Assembly!

Codecademy

Coding is one of the most valuable skills that you can pick up. On the free site, you can choose what to learn, including building websites to analyze data. You’ll learn by doing and can start writing code within a few minutes of joining the site.
Click here to go to Codecademy!

EdX

EdX is an online platform that offers more than 2,500 courses online for free. Take this time to learn a new skill or explore a possible major from institutions like MIT, Harvard, University of California-Berkeley, and more.
Click here to go to EdX!
